# Privacy Policy — Color Tap Challenge

**Effective date:** [13th June 2026]
**Developer:** Phoenix Consultants
**Contact:** [kdstechnology9@gmail.com]

This policy describes what information Color Tap Challenge ("the app", "we", "our") handles and how. The app is designed to operate fully offline; no personal information is collected, transmitted, or stored on our servers.

## What information we collect

**Information you provide:**

The app stores the following on your device only:
- The username you choose on first launch (a short display name, capped at 12 characters).
- Your high score, total coins, highest level reached, and a local Top-5 leaderboard of your runs.
- Your daily login streak progress and today's mission progress.
- Your sound mute preference.

This information is stored in your device's local storage (Android: AsyncStorage / SharedPreferences). It never leaves your device, and we cannot access it. Uninstalling the app removes all of it.

**Information we do not collect:**

- We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information.
- We do not collect location data.
- We do not collect device contacts, photos, or files.
- We do not require account creation or sign-in.
- We do not have a server or backend that receives any data from the app.

## Advertising

The app shows occasional rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. When you tap "Watch Ad to Continue" on the game-over screen, an ad is fetched from Google's network. The ad SDK may collect:

- A reset-able advertising identifier provided by your device (Android Advertising ID)
- Approximate IP-based location for ad delivery
- General device info (model, OS version) used to optimize ad rendering

We have configured the ad request to use non-personalized ads only (requestNonPersonalizedAdsOnly: true), which limits Google's ability to serve targeted ads based on your interests.

For details on how Google handles this data, see Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy and AdMob's data disclosure at https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543.

You can reset or limit your Android Advertising ID at any time:
Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID (or Delete advertising ID to remove it entirely).

## Permissions

The app requests only one permission:
- VIBRATE — used for tactile feedback (haptic vibration on taps and game-over events).

The app explicitly blocks the following permissions even if a third-party SDK requests them:
- Camera
- Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)
- Location (fine and coarse)
- Contacts
- External storage read/write

## Children

The app is rated for general audiences (PEGI 3 / Everyone). It does not knowingly target children under 13. We do not collect any information from any user, including children. If you are under the age in your country at which parental consent is required, please use the app only with permission from a parent or guardian.

## Data retention and deletion

Because all in-app data lives on your device, you control retention by:
- Resetting in-app data: Clear app data via your device's Settings → Apps → Color Tap Challenge → Storage → Clear data.
- Uninstalling: Removes all locally stored data permanently.

We have nothing to delete on our end because we never receive any data.

## Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will update the "Effective date" at the top and post the new version at the same URL. Continuing to use the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
